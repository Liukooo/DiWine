SOMMELIER

We are a group of University students who, like your everyday person, we enjoy accompanying dinner, barbecues,
and different social occasions with a
nice glass of wine. We are firm believers
in what Michael Bradment said, about
how drinking good wine with good food
in good company is one of life’s most
civilized pleasures. This is because wine is more than an alcoholic beverage or fermented grapes.

Meet the Team

Consists of a mix between international and local students that love learning about italian culture and its cuisine. Every single one of us has specific strengths and when we come together we become an unstoppable force.

When one drinks wine, it comes with all
the culture behind it, the history, the multiple similar but completely different flavours
and millions of possible combinations.
This is why we decided to create DiWine
‘The Heavenly Sommelier’. To make this amazing cultural drink more accessible
to people like us so that eventually knowing what wine goes with what food is not something only sommeliers know but something that is a click away.
